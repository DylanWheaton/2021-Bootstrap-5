The Grand Republic Army devised originally as a plan to end the Jedi once and for all by the dark lord who sat at the Republic's helm would serve the Jedi as an elite fighting force that fought well and with loyalty. Under the Jedi they served in battalions each Jedi had control over one battalion that battalion of clones often took on the personalities of their generals and would lay down their lives for them and for the Republic.
